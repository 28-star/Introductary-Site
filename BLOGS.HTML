THE REVOCATION OF ARTICLE 370 : ENHANCEMENT in "RIGHT TO EQUALITY "

Article 370 that provided residents of Jammu and Kashmir with special provisions...After application of this article Constituent assembly posses power to recommend the articles of Indian Constitution, which really gave birth to the feeling of difference between Indians and Kashmiris in the hearts of all.
But after the revocation of this article things have changed...I am confident that the recent changes made in Jammu-Kashmir and Ladakh would be of immense benefit to those regions. They will enable the people to access and enjoy the same rights, same privileges and same facilities as their fellow citizens in the rest of the country. Even this amendment has ensured that Jammu and Kashmir has entered Indian mainstream and has become the part of our great nation's collective growth.So all in all this amendment is a great and appreciating step for the development of the country and its economy along with the establishment of peace, love, hope and dignity among all Indians.
